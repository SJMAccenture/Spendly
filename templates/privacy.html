Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Spendly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have over your data when tracking your personal expenses with us.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic profile information such as your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense entries you log — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you choose to add. We may also collect limited technical information such as your browser type and the times you access the service, which helps us keep Spendly secure and reliable.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Spendly service: to display your expenses, generate category breakdowns and monthly summaries, and let you filter spending by date range. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it to send you marketing messages without your explicit consent.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored on secure servers and protected by industry-standard safeguards. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your associated expense records are removed from our active systems within a reasonable period.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of trusted third-party providers for hosting, error monitoring, and analytics. These providers process data only on our behalf, under agreements that require them to safeguard your information. We do not share your individual expense entries with advertisers or data brokers.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, want to access or delete your data, or wish to raise a privacy concern, please get in touch with us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
